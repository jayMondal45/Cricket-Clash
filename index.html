Rules of the Game:

  • Tie: If you and the computer choose the same option, the round ends in a draw.
  • Winning the Round: The choices follow a cyclic rule:
    • Bat beats Ball
    • Ball beats Stump
    • Stump beats Bat
  • Losing the Round: If the computer's choice defeats your choice based on the cycle above, you lose the round.

Examples

  • You choose Bat, and the computer chooses Ball - You WIN!
  • You choose Ball, and the computer chooses Stump - You WIN!
  • You choose Stump, and the computer chooses Bat - You WIN!
  • You choose Bat, and the computer chooses Stump - You LOSE.
  • You choose Ball, and the computer chooses Bat - You LOSE.
  • You choose Stump, and the computer chooses Ball - You LOSE.
